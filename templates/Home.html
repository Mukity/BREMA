BREMA is a facility that offers the option for the hospitals to post blood requests. The hospitals can manage their own requests and also use the platform to monitor the flow of blood donated by donors and the one transfused to the patients. Through the platform the hospitals are able to tell the blood levels in their Blood storage facility Donors and guests can see blood requests posted by hospitals through the blood request tab. This is a platform that also enables the hospitals to monitor the flow of blood(into the hospital and how much is transfused to patients and when).
